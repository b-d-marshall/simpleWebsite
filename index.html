Ethical Issues in AI and Cybersecurity

Artificial Intelligence and cybersecurity bring powerful benefits, but also ethical challenges.

  • Privacy Concerns: AI systems can collect and analyze personal data, raising concerns about surveillance.
  • Bias in AI: Algorithms can reflect human biases, leading to unfair outcomes.
  • Cybersecurity Threats: Hackers exploit vulnerabilities, risking sensitive data and systems.
  • Job Displacement: Automation powered by AI may replace certain jobs.
