Ethical Issues in AI and Cybersecurity

  • Generative AI poses a lack of effort and/or unoriginality in the game industry.
  • Using generative AI can raise art theft or copyright concerns.

While the lack of cybersecurity is an obvious sour point in online games, the use of generative AI is a more controversial topic.

Due to the strongly negative reception of generative AI in the industry, I would suggest that designers show growth in their artistic skills to find a style that suits them or their game and avoid prominent use of AI generation. If AI is involved, designers should also have the integrity to make sure that their use of AI doesn't steal from existing designs, and take accountability if it does.
